Our Craft

From Petal
to Precious Elixir

In the foothills of rose-valleys and jasmine groves, AURA VALE has pursued a single obsession since its founding — the preservation of nature's most ephemeral beauty in fragrant form.

✦

Rosa Damascena Oil

Harvested at dawn before the dew lifts, our Bulgarian rose petals are cold-pressed within hours of picking — yielding an oil of extraordinary depth and complexity that mass distillation cannot replicate.

✦

Jasmine Night Harvest

True jasmine absolute releases its greatest aromatic intensity after sundown. Our harvest teams work exclusively by moonlight during the three-week flowering season, each bloom hand-selected at peak expression.

✦

Aged Oud Maturation

Our oud is sourced from sustainably certified Aquilaria forests and aged in ceramic vessels for a minimum of seven years. Time is the ingredient that cannot be rushed — or replaced.
